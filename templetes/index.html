About this website

Timely communication with faculty members during examination periods is crucial in educational institutions, particularly for laboratory examinations. However, implementing automated SMS notification systems in India presents challenges due to the high costs of SMS gateways and the complexity of alternative hardware-based mobile integrations. Consequently, institutions often restrict SMS reminders to critical periods only.

This website proposes faculty notification system that delivers automated SMS reminders in a cost-effective, secure, and reliable manner. The system integrates the FAST2SMS gateway, enabling programmatic SMS delivery at minimal cost. An authorized technician uploads and verifies exam-related images or documents through the web application before initiating notifications.
